What Sets Our Tree Services Apart

At LJR Tree Services, we go beyond basic maintenance. Here’s what you can expect when you work with us:

Comprehensive Tree Assessments

Every job starts with a thorough inspection. We take the time to examine the condition of your trees, looking at root health, trunk integrity, branch structure, and overall canopy balance. Whether you’re worried about a leaning tree or just want to make sure your landscape is safe, we give you clear, honest input backed by years of fieldwork.

Tree Pruning with a Purpose

We don’t just cut branches for the sake of it. Proper pruning is essential for tree health and safety, and we approach it with care. Our team knows how to remove dead or overgrown limbs while maintaining the natural shape of your tree. This helps improve airflow, reduce disease risk, and support long-term growth.

Safe and Efficient Tree Removal

Sometimes a tree can’t be saved, whether due to disease, storm damage, or structural instability. In those cases, we’re equipped to remove it with minimal disruption to your property. We plan every step, manage traffic and safety around the site, and always clean up afterward.

Stump Grinding for a Clean Finish

A leftover stump can be both an eyesore and a hazard. We offer stump grinding services to completely remove the remains and prepare your land for new plantings or landscaping. Our crew ensures the process is done neatly and thoroughly.

The Importance of Local Knowledge in Tree Care

Caring for trees in San Jose isn’t the same as caring for trees in other regions. Our local climate presents unique challenges—like limited rainfall, compacted urban soils, and seasonal pests. We bring deep regional expertise to every project. Whether it’s timing your pruning for better growth or identifying fungi that affect California native trees, we base our work on what’s truly needed here—not a generic checklist.

For example, we often see Coast Live Oaks, Redwoods, and ornamental Maples throughout San Jose. Each requires different care strategies. We know how to reduce stress on Oaks in the summer heat or how to thin Redwoods without harming their towering beauty. These aren’t lessons you pick up overnight. They come from years of being out in the field, learning how each tree responds to its environment.

Tree Care That Prioritizes Safety

Tree work can be dangerous—not just for workers, but for your home and neighborhood. That’s why safety is at the core of everything we do. We follow strict safety protocols, carry proper insurance, and make sure our crew is fully trained before they ever climb a tree or operate heavy machinery.

We also take great care to protect your property. That means using mats to avoid damaging your lawn, managing debris to prevent messes, and being mindful of nearby structures, vehicles, and landscaping features. You deserve peace of mind when you hire someone to work on your property, and we work hard to earn that trust.

Our Commitment to Environmental Responsibility

We believe that caring for trees also means caring for the planet. Whenever possible, we promote tree preservation and use pruning techniques that support long-term health rather than short-term appearances. When removal is necessary, we aim to recycle wood and compost debris.

We also share our knowledge with clients. Many homeowners are surprised to learn how tree placement affects water use, energy bills, and even fire safety. We’re happy to offer guidance on what to plant, where to plant it, and how to maintain it sustainably. Our goal isn’t just to finish a job—it’s to leave your landscape in better shape than we found it.
